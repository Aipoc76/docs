# ItemSlot

## DataType:item:ItemSlot

a **Member** of [DataType:item](datatype-item.md)

Usage:

/echo ${FindItem\[Backpack\].ItemSlot}

Return:

\[MQ2\] 23

That means it found a Backpack in slot 23 of your inventory. \(First bag slot\)
