## Examples

Using ${Merchant.Sell\[\#\]}

`/selectitem "Diamond"`

Will select a "Diamond" you can also do "=Diamond" to match EXACT name \(its not case sensitive though\)

Then you can use

`/invoke ${Merchant.Sell[100]}`

Sells 100 of whatever is selected with /selectitem
